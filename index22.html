About Beirut Poly

Beirut Polytechnic is a forward-thinking technical institution dedicated to empowering students with practical skills and innovative thinking. Located in the vibrant city of Beirut, we offer a range of engineering, computer science, and design programs tailored to meet the demands of a rapidly evolving global market.

Our mission is to bridge theory with hands-on application, fostering a new generation of professionals ready to lead in technology and industry.
